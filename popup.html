SafeWish

Wishlist Dox-Blocker

Protection Active Monitoring Amazon product pages

What does SafeWish do?

Amazon now allows third-party sellers to fulfill wishlist orders — meaning they receive your full shipping address.

SafeWish scans every product page and warns you before you add a third-party item to your wishlist, so your address stays private.

🔒 100% client-side — zero data leaves your browser
🚫 No APIs, no tracking, no analytics
⚡ Lightweight — less than 8KB total
🌐 Works on all Amazon country domains
